WordPress Integration for Hiveify

== Description ==

This plugin connects your WordPress website with Hiveify, a powerful email delivery platform.

Hiveify offers robust functionalities for efficient subscriber and list management, seamless email campaigns, and precise message tracking, all from a modern and flexible interface. Utilize Hiveify to send both transactional and marketing emails, and generate detailed reports. This scalable solution ensures superior email deliverability, allowing you to focus on growing your business without worrying about the complexities of email infrastructure.
